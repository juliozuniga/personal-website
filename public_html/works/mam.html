MAM (2018):

for amplified flute, cello, clarinet, and electronics

written in collaboration with Madison Greenstone, Michael Matsuno, and T.J. Borden, who over the course of a year came together as DAD to make this project happen

audio:

studio recording, UC San Diego, 2018/10/14
Alexandria Smith, recording engineer; Murat Çolak at GERYON, mastering engineer; mixed by me

score

score/audio:

This piece was written in stages over the past year. The version you will hear tonight attempts to merge and expand what began as a series of possible listenings or zoom-ins on a white noise section at the center of GIS, another recent piece of mine.

I am extremely grateful to Michael, Madison, and TJ, who made this project a reality. This work is dedicated to them and to my second mom, Tatis, who, in her backyard, inadvertently taught me how to listen.

Program note from the premiere at Harvard, 2018/9/19
